Year: 1904
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 158.8 x 108 cm

Description: This painting depicts a woman standing in a poised and elegant manner. The woman is dressed in a formal gown with a black bodice and a white, off-the-shoulder neckline. She is adorned with a long, gold necklace that drapes down the front of her dress. Over her shoulders, she wears a pink, satin-like shawl that adds a touch of color and texture to her attire. Her hair is styled in an elaborate updo, and she stands with one hand on her hip and the other resting on a stone balustrade. The background features rich, dark red drapery and a stone column, adding to the opulent and sophisticated atmosphere of the painting. The painting is interesting and relevant as it showcases the fashion and elegance of a bygone era, capturing the grace and refinement of the subject.
