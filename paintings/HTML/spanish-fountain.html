Year: 1912
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: watercolor
Dimensions: 53.3 x 34.9 cm

Description: This watercolor painting captures the splendor of a grand fountain. The fountain features an elegant basin from which water gracefully flows through a spout. Below the basin, sculpted figures of cherubs or children provide decorative support, adding a charming touch to the design. The water cascades down into a pool below, creating delicate ripples on the surface. The artist skillfully portrays the texture of the stone and the fluidity of the water, with a keen eye for light and shadow. This artwork beautifully showcases the artist's ability to capture the intricacies and dynamic movement of the fountain.
