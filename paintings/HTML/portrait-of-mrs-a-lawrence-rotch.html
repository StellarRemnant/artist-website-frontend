Year: 1903
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 114.2 x 92.1 cm

Description: This painting portrays a woman seated gracefully. She is adorned in a light blue dress with intricate lace details, which drape elegantly over her seated form. Her right arm rests gently on a surface, while the left is gracefully positioned with the fabric of her dress cascading down. A large, elaborate hat with feathers sits atop her head, adding a touch of elegance to her attire. Her necklace, made of blue beads, adds to her refined appearance. The background is dark, which accentuates the light hues of her dress and hat, drawing the viewer's focus directly to her. The artist has captured the delicate textures of the fabric and the subtle interplay of light and shadow, highlighting the woman's poise and sophistication.
