Year: 1882
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 81.9 x 59.7 cm

Description: The painting depicts a woman seated against a dark, textured background. She is dressed in a black garment with a deep V-neckline, and her face is obscured. She holds a fan with a dark base and vibrant red and blue patterns. The background and her attire create a stark contrast with the bright colors of the fan and the subtle highlights on her skin and hair. The use of dark tones and the vibrant fan make the painting visually striking and suggest a sense of mystery or elegance.
