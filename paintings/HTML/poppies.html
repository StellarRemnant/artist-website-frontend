Year: 1886
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 61.91 x 91.12 cm

Description: The artwork offers a striking and lively portrayal of a poppy field. The artist uses bold, sweeping brushstrokes to render the flowers, giving the scene a sense of motion and energy. The brilliant red of the poppies contrasts beautifully with the more muted greens and shadows of the surrounding foliage, drawing the viewer’s eye and conveying a sense of natural vitality. The play of light and shadow throughout the piece adds depth and realism, while the impressionistic approach gives the scene an expressive and fleeting quality. Overall, Sargent’s depiction of the poppies is both mesmerizing and emotional, demonstrating his skill with the Impressionist style.
