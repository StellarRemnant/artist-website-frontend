Year: 1885–86
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 174.0 cm × 153.7 cm

Description: The painting depicts two small children dressed in white who are lighting paper lanterns as day turns to evening; they are in a garden strewn with pink roses, accents of yellow carnations and tall white lilies (possibly the Japanese mountain lily, Lilium auratum) behind them. The painting is dominated by green foliage, with no horizon or other horizontal line to give a sense of depth.
