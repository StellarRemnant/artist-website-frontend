Year: 1913
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 49.6 x 71.8 cm

Description: The painting depicts a serene scene on a rocky shore. A woman dressed in a long, light-colored dress stands facing the water, holding a fishing rod. The body of water is depicted with various shades of blue and green, adding depth and movement to the scene. Several ducks swim peacefully near the shore, enhancing the tranquil atmosphere. The artist's skillful use of light and color creates a vivid and dynamic representation of this natural setting, capturing the beauty and quiet moments of everyday life. The painting is interesting for its calm and peaceful depiction of nature, emphasizing the stillness and serenity of the moment.
