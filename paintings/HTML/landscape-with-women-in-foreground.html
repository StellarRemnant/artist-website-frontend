Year: 1883
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 63.5 x 77.5 cm

Description: The painting depicts a serene outdoor scene with two women standing on a grassy path surrounded by trees. The women are dressed in long, period-style dresses, and one of them is pointing towards a flowering tree on the right side of the image. The background features a mix of dense foliage and a sunlit clearing, creating a peaceful and picturesque setting. The painting captures the beauty of nature and the leisurely activity of the women, making it an interesting and relevant depiction of a tranquil moment in a natural environment.
