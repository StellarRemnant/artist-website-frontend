Year: 1907
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 152.7 x 101.6 cm

Description: The painting is of a woman in a white dress with pink accents. The background is a blend of soft, pastel colors, primarily blues and pinks, giving a dreamy and ethereal atmosphere. The woman is depicted in a three-quarter view, with her head turned slightly to the side. The painting is signed by the artist, John Singer Sargent, in the top left corner. The date "1907" is visible in the top right corner. This painting showcases Sargent's skill in capturing the delicate textures of fabric and the subtle play of light and shadow.
