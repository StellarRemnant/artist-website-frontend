Year: 1905
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Charcoal on paper
Dimensions: 61 x 47 cm

Description: This charcoal drawing depicts Eugenia Errázuriz, the wife of the Chilean landscape painter José Tomás Errázuriz. The artwork captures intricate details, especially in her elaborate hat and jewelry, with skillful use of shading and light to enhance the textures. The focus on her attire and accessories, while her face is slightly blurred, adds an element of mystery and highlights the artist's intentional emphasis. The overall composition is both dramatic and elegant, evoking a sense of depth and sophistication.
