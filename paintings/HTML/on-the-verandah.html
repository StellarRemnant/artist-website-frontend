Year: 1921-1922
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: watercolor
Dimensions: 39.7 x 53.3 cm

Description: This watercolor painting captures a serene scene on a porch. The porch, supported by white columns, overlooks a lush garden filled with blooming flowers. In the painting, four individuals are depicted: one person is seated on the floor on the left side, two people are seated in chairs in the middle, and one person is seated on a bench on the right side. The individuals appear to be engaged in leisurely activities, such as knitting or relaxing. The painting exudes a sense of peace and tranquility, highlighting a leisurely moment in a rural or suburban setting. The artist's use of light and color enhances the calm and inviting atmosphere of the scene.
