Year: 1879
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 34.9 × 26 cm

Description: The painting captures a vibrant Moroccan street scene. The street is narrow, flanked by tall, white-washed buildings that reflect the bright sunlight. The architecture features traditional Moroccan elements, with arched doorways and intricately designed windows. The cobblestone path leads the eye through the composition, creating a sense of depth and perspective. Small details, such as hanging lanterns and colorful textiles, add a lively atmosphere to the scene. The background reveals a clear blue sky, contrasting beautifully with the white buildings. This painting beautifully showcases the charm and cultural richness of a Moroccan street, immersing the viewer in its lively ambiance.
