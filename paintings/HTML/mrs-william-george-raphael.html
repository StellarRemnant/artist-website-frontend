Year: 1906
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 142.2 × 104.1 cm

Description: Mrs. William George Raphael is a work of stunning subtlety and balance, and a testament to Sargent’s achievements in portraiture during this period. Using a delicate range of light and color, the artist captures the tone and texture of his model’s diaphanous dress. His use of vigorously applied impressionistic brushstrokes exemplifies his expertise in capturing the fleeting effects of light. The bright white highlights of her dress are reflected harmoniously in her jewelry, while the rose gold tones of her hair are set off by the warm highlights in the side table.
