Year: 1907
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: watercolor
Dimensions: 50.8 x 60.96 cm

Description: This watercolor painting depicts a serene harbor scene. In the foreground, there is a person sitting in a small boat, wearing a hat and a blue garment. The boat is painted with soft, flowing strokes, and the water around it reflects the colors of the sky and the boats. In the background, several large ships with tall masts and rigging are painted in a loose, impressionistic style. These ships are docked near a waterfront with buildings that suggest a European coastal town. The architecture and atmosphere evoke a sense of calm and peacefulness. The use of watercolor gives the scene a dreamy, ethereal quality, emphasizing the interplay of light and color on the water and the boats. This painting beautifully captures the tranquil atmosphere of the harbor, making it an interesting and visually appealing artwork.
