Year: 1910
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: watercolor
Dimensions: 53.2 x 40.5 cm

Description: This painting depicts a serene garden scene with lush greenery and classical sculptures. In the foreground, there's a stone balustrade adorned with two large statues of seated figures, possibly lions or mythological creatures, bathed in sunlight. These statues flank the balustrade, drawing the viewer's eye toward the background. On the left side, several large potted plants with vibrant green foliage cast shadows on the ground. The background is dominated by a dense, dark canopy of trees, creating a striking contrast with the brightly lit foreground. The interplay of light and shadow, along with the detailed rendering of the statues and plants, adds depth and realism to the scene, making it an engaging and visually appealing composition.
