Year: 1906
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 110.6 x 86.5cm

Description: This elegant portrait captures Lady Eden seated at a table, deeply engaged in a card game. She wears a sophisticated black gown with an off-the-shoulder neckline and a delicate cashmere shawl draped over her shoulders. Her right hand holds a few cards, while her left hand rests on the table, interacting with the cards laid out. The richly adorned table and the ornate urn in the background highlight the luxurious setting. The artist skillfully uses light and shadow to emphasize the textures of her attire and the surrounding objects, creating a visually captivating and refined scene.
