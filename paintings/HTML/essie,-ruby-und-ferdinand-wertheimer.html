Year: 1902
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 161.3 x 193.7 cm

Description: The painting depicts three children along with two dogs. The children are positioned on and around a large bed with white and red bedding. The child on the left is wearing a white dress with red accents and has a red bow in their hair. The child on the right is dressed in a dark suit and is leaning on the bed. The third child is reclining on the bed, partially obscured by the bedding. The two dogs are positioned near the children, one on the left side of the image and the other on the right side of the bed. The painting is characterized by its rich use of color and detailed brushwork, capturing a moment of leisure and companionship.
