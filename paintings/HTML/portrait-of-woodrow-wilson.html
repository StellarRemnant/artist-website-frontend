Year: 1917
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 153 x 109.2 cm

Description: This portrait of Woodrow Wilson, the President of the United States at the time, captures him seated in a chair, dressed in a dark suit with a vest and tie. His right hand rests on the arm of the chair, while his left hand is placed on his lap. The background is dark, which makes Wilson's figure stand out prominently. The painting is signed by the artist, John Singer Sargent, in the top left corner, and the year "1917" is written in the top right corner.
