Description: Sargent captures a woman standing with an air of grace and confidence. She wears a long, elegant gray dress with a deep V-neckline and a lace-up bodice. The dress features puffed sleeves adorned with white lace trim at the cuffs, adding a touch of sophistication. The woman holds a white flower, possibly a magnolia, in her left hand, which contrasts beautifully with the dark, shadowy background. The play of light and shadow emphasizes the texture of her dress and the delicate petals of the flower, drawing attention to her figure.
