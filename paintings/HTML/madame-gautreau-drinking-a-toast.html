Year: 1882
Art movement: Realism
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 32 x 41 cm

Description: The painting depicts a woman seated at a table, extending her right arm to hold a glass. The background is dark, and there are some light-colored flowers on the table near her arm. The style of the painting is impressionistic, with visible brush strokes and a focus on light and shadow. The artist's signature, "John S. Sargent," is visible in the upper right corner of the painting. This artwork is interesting due to its dramatic and intimate atmosphere, created by the use of light and shadow, as well as the distinctive brushwork of the artist.
