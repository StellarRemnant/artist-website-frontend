Year: 1917
Art movement: Impressionism
Medium: watercolor
Dimensions: 25.4 x 35.6 cm

Description: This watercolor painting depicts a serene coastal scene. The painting features a rocky shoreline with water gently flowing and reflecting light. The colors used are predominantly cool tones such as blues, greens, and purples, with hints of warm tones like yellows and browns. The brushstrokes are loose and fluid, capturing the movement and texture of the water and rocks. This image evokes a sense of tranquility and natural beauty through its impressionistic style.
